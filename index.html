POEM para kay Angelaaaa

A girl i met by accident
She is adorable like the sunsets,
Beautiful like the moon,
But very far to reach like the stars

I watch her smile from the world away,
Like the waves admire the sky each day.
She doesn't know the tide she stirs
In silent hearts and whispered words.
Her pretty eyes, her pretty smile,
and her good heart make me fall for her each day.

Even if i saw her flaws,
I would still choose her a million times.
I like her, and I will admire her
Always, in all ways.
